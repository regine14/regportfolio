About me

I am a highly motivated scientist with hands-on expertise in wet lab techniques. Having at least 8 years of research experience, I am trained with molecular biology skills and experienced in integrating wet lab techniques with computational approaches to identify novel therapeutic targets and biomarkers. My passion for science has led me to travel across the globe - From home in Malaysia, to UK and now a PR in Singapore.

By the way, check out my awesome work.
